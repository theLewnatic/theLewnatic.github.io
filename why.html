Learn more on Why we choose to help the poverty striken in India

In india 22% of its population, that's 270 million people, are living on less than $1.25 a day. 45% of those people are illiterate, 94% don't have access to tap water,and 39% dont have electricity. We at Pot for Poverty want to use the economy of India itself to begin to tackle the overwhleming needs of the poor. India is a land in need in more ways than one, and our vision is one that uses buisness to solve social and economic issues around the world. Pot for Poverty is above all a reflection of those values that we hold dear.

We chose to focus our efforts on education as a "teach a man to fish" apporach to long term aid for these impoverished communites. We focus on educating the youth to help them rise to their potential and tackle the staggering illiteracy rate amongst the poor. All of this in efforts to solve the global plague of poverty where it lies, and bring the condition of the poor to a sustainable one.
